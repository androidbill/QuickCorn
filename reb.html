About QuickCorn

This rebuild keeps the original idea, but the app logic is now state-driven and the tournament engine is deterministic.

Built as a single mobile-first HTML file.
Features included: 1v1 / 2v2, ACL or total scoring, round editing, team colors, player PPR, and double-elimination tournament play for 2–8 teams.
